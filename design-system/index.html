VERSEN
Design system

Premium curated commerce.

Versen ska kännas som en veckovis redaktionell produktedit med checkout ansluten. Systemet prioriterar mobil, produktens studio-närvaro, mjuk typografi och få tydliga handlingar.

Global rules

Luxury minimal, never template ecommerce.

Every reusable pattern should feel calm, cinematic, curated and product-led. Avoid marketplace density, loud sale mechanics, generic product grids and gym-shop energy.

01

Warm white canvas

Use soft off-white backgrounds, white cards, restrained lines and almost no cold gray.

02

Product as subject

Transparent cutouts sit on pedestal shadows with soft lighting and isolated studio focus.

03

Editorial hierarchy

Serif headlines carry emotion. Sans UI stays quiet, useful and precise.

04

One clear action

Each screen chooses a primary path and lets secondary controls recede.

Menu rules

Navigation can feel cinematic.

The overlay should use black as a stage, large sparse links, and one editorial product promo. Avoid many columns, icons beside every link, or account clutter.

Mobile-first rules

The phone is the primary storefront.

Design the mobile flow first. Desktop expands the same editorial rhythm; it does not introduce extra clutter.
